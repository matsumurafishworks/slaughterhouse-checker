🔴 Non-Stun Religious Slaughter {{ non_stun|length }} establishments

These abattoirs are certified by the Halal Monitoring Committee (HMC) for non-stun halal slaughter. Animals are not stunned before killing. Source: HMC certified supplier PDF, cross-referenced against FSA approval numbers.

⚠️ This is a confirmed minimum, not the full picture. The FSA's own 2024 Slaughter Sector Survey identified approximately 24 red meat abattoirs carrying out non-stun slaughter in England and Wales alone — meaning at least 11 non-stun sites are not captured here. Additional non-stun poultry sites exist beyond that. The gap exists because:
  • Not all non-stun halal operations use HMC — some use smaller or informal certification bodies with no public lists
  • All Kosher (Shechita) sites are non-stun but KLBD do not publish FSA approval codes
  • The FSA has withheld per-abattoir non-stun data in response to FOI requests
We have written to the HFA and KLBD requesting their certified abattoir lists. This figure will be updated if they respond.

📬 Help Us Improve

This dataset is constantly updated as new information becomes available. We cross-reference public sources monthly — but the full picture requires information that certification bodies and the FSA currently withhold.

What we're missing

  • Non-HMC certified non-stun halal sites
  • Kosher (Shechita) certified abattoirs with FSA codes
  • Smaller regional certification body lists
  • Sites that have changed their slaughter method
  • Scotland and NI religious slaughter data

How to submit data

If you have information that would improve this dataset — an abattoir's FSA code and its slaughter method, a certification body list, or a correction to existing data — please get in touch.

All submissions are reviewed before being added.
